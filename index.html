Learn more about our farm

We specialize in 'hands-on' experiences for all the family, ranging from bottle feeding the lambs and baby goats, cuddling the rabbits and guinea pigs in Pets' Corner and grooming the ponies.

As we are an organic working farm, farming both dairy cattle and arable crops, visitors also have the opportunity to discover where their food comes from, through experiences such as milking parlour demonstrations and planting activities.
